Workshop Submissions

Full Workshop Submissions
All submissions must be original research not under review at any other venue. Solicited submissions include both full technical workshop papers and white position papers. Maximum length of such submissions is 6 pages including references, and if accepted they will be published by ACM and appear in the ACM Digital Library.

Work-in-Progress and Demo Submissions
Abstracts describing work-in-progress and demonstrations are also welcome and warmly encouraged.
